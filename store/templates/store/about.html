About Us

At Transdigital Hospital Products Store, we specialize in providing a wide range of essential and advanced medical supplies to hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and individuals across Kenya. Our goal is to ensure that high-quality healthcare products are accessible, affordable, and conveniently available online.

Our Product Range

  • 🩺 Diagnostic Equipment – Blood pressure monitors, thermometers, oximeters, stethoscopes.
  • 💉 Medical Consumables – Syringes, gloves, gauze, catheters, and IV supplies.
  • 🏥 Hospital Furniture – Examination beds, stretchers, IV stands, wheelchairs.
  • 🧼 Sanitation & PPE – Face masks, hand sanitizers, disinfectants, gowns, face shields.
  • 🔬 Lab Equipment & Supplies – Microscopes, test tubes, reagents, specimen containers.
  • 🛒 Home Care Products – First aid kits, orthopedic aids, diabetic kits, and mobility aids.
